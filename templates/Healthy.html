Healthy Cotton Boll

To keep the cotton bolls healthy,follow these recommendations:

  • Water the cotton plants regularly and deeply, but avoid overwatering or flooding them.
  • Apply balanced fertilizers and organic matter to improve soil quality and nutrient availability.
  • Monitor and control pests and diseases that can damage the bolls and reduce yield.
  • Harvest the bolls when they are fully mature and dry, and store them in a cool and dry place.
